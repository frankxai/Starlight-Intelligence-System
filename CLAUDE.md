## What is Starlight?

**Starlight Intelligence System** is the unified intelligence backbone for the entire FrankX ecosystem. It is the meta-layer that orchestrates, remembers, learns, and connects all other systems.

When you open Claude Code in this directory, you get:

- **7 Starlight Agents** - Purpose-built intelligence personas
- **16+ Skills** - Auto-activate via `skill-rules.json`
- **6 Commands** - Strategic intelligence operations
- **Starlight Vaults** - Persistent memory across sessions and repos
- **Starlight Notes** - Knowledge capture and synthesis
- **Starlight Transmissions** - Cross-repo intelligence communication
- **Context Engine** - Unified awareness across the ecosystem
